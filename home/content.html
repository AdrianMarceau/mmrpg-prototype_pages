What is the Mega Man RPG Prototype?

After a freak accident Dr. Light and Mega Man find themselves digitized, separated from Dr. Cossack, and trapped in a prototype battle simulator! The two heroes are forced to fight for their lives as they search for their friend and a way back to the real world! Challenge powered-up copies of past robots to battle and download their data to become stronger and escape from the system!

The Mega Man RPG Prototype is an ongoing fangame project with the goal of creating a progress-saving, no-download, no-install, cross-platform, browser-based Mega Man RPG (or what some would call a PBBG) that combines the addictive collection and battle mechanics of the Pok√©mon series with the beloved robots and special weapons of the classic Mega Man series. Fight your way through an army of robot masters in a turn-based battle system reminiscent of both play-by-post forum games and early 8-bit role-playing games.

This project is a labour of love and a massive work-in-progress, but by creating a new account you'll gain access to the full game with save functionality, 42 unique battle fields, over 1000 dynamically generated missions, 3 playable characters, 60+ unlockable robots, 160+ abilities, 90+ items, and tons of interesting features and mechanics. Additional information about the development of the game can be found on the About, Credits, and Database pages, and discussions about the game can be found on the Community pages. We look forward to seeing you online, and don't forget to pop into our Discord channel so we can say hello!
